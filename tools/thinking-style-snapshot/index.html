    This snapshot is not a diagnosis or a measure of intelligence or worth. It’s a quick pattern-check and a conversation starter.

    How to read your snapshot

    Count how many YES answers you gave.

    • 0–3 YES → Focused Thinker (depth, consistency, sustained attention)
    • 4–7 YES → Hybrid Thinker (balances depth with variety; switches modes on purpose)
    • 8–10 YES → Broad / Systems Thinker (thrives on novelty; connects ideas across domains)

    Multipotentialite context (optional)

    This quiz grew out of the “multipotentialite” conversation: people who have many interests and tend to learn fast, connect ideas, and change lanes over time. If you want the vocabulary (and the reassurance), start here:

    • Puttylike: What is a multipotentialite?
    • TED-Ed lesson: “Why some of us don’t have one true calling”
    • Multipotentiality (Wikipedia)
    • How to Be Everything (book info)
    • Refuse to Choose (Scanner mind) (book info)

    What to do with your result

    • Focused: double down on one skill and track progress weekly.
    • Hybrid: plan “focus blocks” + “explore blocks” on purpose.
    • Broad/Systems: keep a backlog of ideas and choose one “main quest” per month.
    Tip: treat this as an experiment. Re-take after a big life change or every 6–12 months.
