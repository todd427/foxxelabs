About Foxxe Labs

Foxxe Labs is a small workshop where ideas become prototypes and prototypes become tools. The goal is simple: build things that make thinking easier, learning faster, and choices clearer.

What you’ll find here

  • Tools you can share (quizzes, checklists, small interactive demos).
  • Research-adjacent prototypes that explore human-AI interaction, trust, and cognition.
  • Creative experiments that are useful even when they’re strange.

What you won’t find (unless a page explicitly says otherwise)

  • Hidden data collection.
  • Tracking by default.
If a tool ever collects responses, the page will say so clearly, and will include a plain-language privacy note.
